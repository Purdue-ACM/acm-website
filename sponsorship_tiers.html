Sponsorship Tiers

  • General Perks:

      • Your company’s Logo displayed in the appropriate tier on ACM’s website (acm.cs.purdue.edu)

    • Company Logo on robots’ side panels
    • Company Logo on website (purduesigbots.com)
  • Event Perks (If donating to an event):
    • Company Logo on promotional flyers
