Sponsorship Tiers

  • General Perks:
    • Silver (< $500)
      • Your company’s Logo displayed in the appropriate tier on ACM’s website (acm.cs.purdue.edu)
    • Gold (< $1000)
      • All of Silver’s perks
      • Resume book from all of ACM
    • Platinum (>= $1000)
      • All of Gold’s perks
      • Company Logo on ACM’s display board in Lawson Computer Science Building
  • SIGBots Perks (for donations marked for SIGBots’ requests):
    • Company Logo on team t-shirts
    • Company Logo on robots’ side panels
    • Company Logo on website (purduesigbots.com)
  • Event Perks (If donating to an event):
    • Company Logo on promotional flyers
